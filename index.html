Letter

1.What is letter?

Ans:-Letters are the individual characters that are used to represent sounds in a written form.In english alphabet there are 26 letters.

Vowels and semi-vowels

1.What is vowels and write its examples?

Ans:-A vowel is a letter or sound that can be spoken independently. In english alphabet a,e,i,o,u are vowels.

2.What is semi-vowel and write its examples?

Ans:-A semi-vowel is a letter and sound that is a produced like a vowel but functions as a consonant.In, english alphabet, y and w are semi-vowels.

Consonants

1.What is consonant and write its examples?

Ans:-A consonant is a letter or sound that can be spoken with the help of a vowel or semi-vowel only.In, english grammar b,c,d,f,g,h,j,k, l,m,n,p,q,r,s,t,v,w,x,y,z are consonants.
